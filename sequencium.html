How to play

Each player starts with a short diagonal chain of numbers in opposite corners. On your turn, place the next number in your chain on any empty square touching your highest number - up, down, sideways, or diagonally. Available squares are coloured with the respective player's colour. If you have no legal move, your turn is skipped. Once the board fills up completely, whoever built the longer chain wins.
